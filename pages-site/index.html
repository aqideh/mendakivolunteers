Platform principles

Designed to supplement YM Hub

Secure identity boundary

Portal accounts remain separate from the authoritative YM Hub volunteer identifier until controlled linking occurs.

App-owned content

Opportunity discovery and volunteer news are managed in the portal without taking ownership of registration records.

Database-enforced access

Role checks, row-level security, audit history, and server-only credentials underpin the full application.
